About Us

Inspired by Sophie

Sophie's Homecare was founded in loving memory of Sophie, a gentle soul whose warmth and compassion continue to inspire everything we do.

Our vision was to create more than just a care facility — but a nurturing home where seniors are treated with dignity, patience, kindness, and respect.

At Sophie's Homecare, compassionate care is at the heart of everything we do.

Our Mission

Our mission at Sophie's Homecare is to provide compassionate, personalized care that honors the dignity and individuality of every resident. We are committed to creating a safe, peaceful, and nurturing home where seniors receive the support they need while maintaining comfort, independence, and quality of life. Inspired by the memory of Sophie, our care is guided by kindness, patience, and respect.

What We Offer

Our Services

Sophie's Homecare provides personalized senior care in a small residential setting designed to offer comfort, safety, and individual attention. Our small 6-resident home allows caregivers to provide more attentive and individualized care.

Assisted Living Support

Support with daily living activities including bathing, dressing, grooming, mobility assistance, and medication reminders — so your loved one can maintain their independence with confidence.

Hospice & Non-Ambulatory Care

Compassionate care for residents requiring higher levels of support, including hospice collaboration and end-of-life care — ensuring dignity, comfort, and family involvement every step of the way.

Post-Surgery Recovery

A calm and supportive environment for seniors recovering after surgery or hospitalization — with personalized recovery plans to help them return to daily life sooner.

Respite Care

Short-term stays available for families who need temporary care for their loved ones — giving you peace of mind while we provide the same quality care.

Daily Living Services

Medication management, meal preparation, housekeeping, laundry, mobility assistance, social engagement, and 24/7 supervision — creating a structured yet comfortable routine that feels like home.
