# About Us

Our vision was to create more than just a care facility — but a nurturing home where seniors are treated with dignity, patience, kindness, and respect.

At Sophie’s Homecare, compassionate care is at the heart of everything we do.

# Mission Statement

Our mission at Sophie’s Homecare is to provide compassionate, personalized care that honors the dignity and individuality of every resident.

We are committed to creating a safe, peaceful, and nurturing home where seniors receive the support they need while maintaining comfort, independence, and quality of life.

Inspired by the memory of Sophie, our care is guided by kindness, patience, and respect.

# Services

## Personalized Residential Care

Sophie’s Homecare provides personalized senior care in a small residential setting designed to offer comfort, safety, and individual attention.

---

## Highest Caregiver-to-Resident Ratio

Our small 6-resident home allows caregivers to provide more attentive and individualized care compared to larger facilities.

Residents receive consistent support, companionship, and supervision throughout the day.

---

## Assisted Living Support

Support with daily living activities including:

- Bathing
- Dressing
- Grooming
- Mobility assistance
- Medication reminders

---

## Hospice & Non-Ambulatory Care

Compassionate care for residents requiring higher levels of support, including hospice collaboration and end-of-life care.

---

## Post-Surgery Recovery

A calm and supportive environment for seniors recovering after surgery or hospitalization.

---

## Respite Care

Short-term stays available for families who need temporary care for their loved ones.

---

## Daily Living Services

Services include:

- Medication management
- Meal preparation and nutrition
- Housekeeping and laundry
- Mobility assistance
- Social engagement
- 24/7 supervision and care

# Website Color Theme

The website should use a calm, warm, and welcoming color palette that reflects compassion, trust, and a homelike environment.

---

## Primary Color — Lavender

HEX: #8E6BD3

Usage:

- Buttons
- Section titles
- Icons
- Highlights

---

## Secondary Color — Warm Beige

HEX: #E6D9CC

Usage:

- Website background
- Section backgrounds
- Soft content blocks

This color creates a warm residential feeling rather than a clinical environment.

---

## Accent Color — Light Lavender

HEX: #B9A6E8

Usage:

- Divider lines
- Decorative icons
- Hover effects
- Subtle accents

---

## Text Color — Charcoal / Dark Navy

HEX: #1C1C2E

Usage:

- Headings
- Paragraph text
- Navigation

Provides strong readability and professional appearance.

---

## Optional Accent — Sage Green

HEX: #8FAF9B

Usage:

- Secondary highlights
- Wellness elements
- Icon accents

Represents healing, peace, and comfort.
